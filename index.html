Who am I?

Studious Software and STEM engineer by day. Avid game designer by night. Hi, my name is Theo Kim. I attended UC San Diego where I graduated with a BS in Structural Engineering. After working peripherally with amazing software and robotic automation in aerospace, I felt a wanderlust to travel off the beaten path and dive into a new field, filled with new ideas, paradigms, and possibilities.

I enrolled into an immersive development bootcamp with Actualize. I fell in love with coding, and have been immersing myself in the world of software development since. My directive is to one day help develop software that will provide future generations achieve fulfillment and happiness.
